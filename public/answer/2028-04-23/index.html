📖 Meaning medium

金字招牌 (jīn zì zhāo pái)

A symbol of high reputation and trustworthiness, often used for a brand or person with an impeccable image.

Literal: gold character signboard

💡 Example

After decades of excellent service, the store became a gold-lettered signboard in the community.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

This idiom is about something that is like a seal of quality or a guarantee of excellence.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

The first character represents a precious metal, and the last two characters refer to a shop sign.
