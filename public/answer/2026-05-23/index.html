📖 Meaning medium

息息相关 (xī xī xiāng guān)

Two things are closely related and mutually dependent.

Literal: each breath related

💡 Example

Their success is closely tied to the team's effort, like two things intertwined.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

This phrase describes an inseparable connection, like how breathing sustains life.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

The first character repeats, meaning 'each' or 'every', and relates to inhalation and exhalation.
