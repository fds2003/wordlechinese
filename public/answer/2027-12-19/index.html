📖 Meaning medium

一脉相承 (yī mài xiāng chéng)

A continuous line of inheritance or transmission from one generation to the next; consistent in tradition.

Literal: one pulse mutual inherit

💡 Example

The architectural style of this temple is one continuous line from the Tang dynasty, a 一脉相承 of design.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

Think about family lineage or traditions passed down through time.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

The first character means 'one' and the third suggests 'mutual'.
