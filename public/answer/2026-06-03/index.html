📖 Meaning medium

名副其实 (míng fù qí shí)

The name matches the reality; to be worthy of the name.

Literal: name matches its reality

💡 Example

His achievements are truly worthy of his reputation as a top scientist.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

Think about someone who is exactly as good as their title suggests.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

It involves the idea of 'name' and 'reality' being in agreement.
