📖 Meaning medium

杯水车薪 (bēi shuǐ chē xīn)

Using a cup of water to put out a cartload of burning firewood; an utterly inadequate effort to solve a large problem.

Literal: cup of water, cartload of firewood

💡 Example

His small donation was like a drop in the bucket, a case of cup of water to a cartload of firewood.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

Think about an action that is too small to make a difference.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

One character refers to liquid in a drinking vessel.
