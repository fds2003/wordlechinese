📖 Meaning medium

转危为安 (zhuǎn wēi wéi ān)

To turn danger into safety; to avert a crisis.

Literal: turn danger into safety

💡 Example

Quick thinking and teamwork helped the company turn the crisis around and turn danger into safety.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

This idiom describes a positive outcome after a bad situation.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

Think about changing a risky state to a secure one.
