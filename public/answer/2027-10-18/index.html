📖 Meaning medium

首当其冲 (shǒu dāng qí chōng)

To bear the brunt of an attack or misfortune.

Literal: head, act as, its, rush/charge

💡 Example

During the crisis, the small businesses were the first to bear the brunt.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

This idiom is often used when talking about being the first to face a disaster or challenge.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

The second character means 'to act as' or 'to serve as'.
