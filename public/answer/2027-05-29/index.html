📖 Meaning medium

水到渠成 (shuǐ dào qú chéng)

When conditions are ripe, success will come naturally.

Literal: water arrives, channel forms

💡 Example

Don't force it; with hard work and patience, things will naturally fall into place like water flowing into a channel.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

Think about a natural process where patience leads to a perfect outcome.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

The second character suggests 'arrival' or 'reaching'.
