📖 Meaning easy

坚持不懈 (jiān chí bù xiè)

To persistently and unremittingly continue doing something without giving up.

Literal: persist, hold, not, slack

💡 Example

She studied English for years with unremitting effort, never giving up despite challenges.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

This idiom describes a quality needed for long-term goals.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

It combines the ideas of holding on and never loosening.
