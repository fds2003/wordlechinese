📖 Meaning easy

足不出户 (zú bù chū hù)

It means to stay at home all the time, never going out. It describes someone who rarely leaves their house.

Literal: foot not go out door

💡 Example

Since he started working from home, he has been living a life of 'zu bu chu hu'.

🎯 Hints (for next time)

Hint 1 — Subtle

This idiom describes a lifestyle of being extremely homebound.

Hint 2 — More Obvious

The last character means 'door' or 'household'.
